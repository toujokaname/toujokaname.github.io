Idol Story 1

Season: Spring

Location: CosPro Office

—Are you looking for a ghost or something?

Ah, apologies, HiMERU has startled you. No good, no good. Nowadays, he tends to infringe on what is forbidden.

"Don't talk to people from behind", "especially women", "and in dark places"—

This will not do. Perhaps HiMERU is losing focus. Because as they say, "the most dangerous time is when you become accustomed to something".

...Oops. "Muttering to yourself" is no good, either.

—Once again, greetings! This is HiMERU!

He belongs to Cosmic Production, as you may be aware of... not? Aah, really?

HiMERU has been active as a solo artist for a long time, and he is very proud of the results he has achieved.

Well, "units" are in vogue these days, so no matter what solo idols do or where they are, they don't attract much attention.

—Very informative. Thank you, HiMERU has acquired new knowledge.

...Oh dear? Has HiMERU perplexed you?

"When the other party doesn't reply, it means they are at a loss"—right, apologies!

Fufu. HiMERU has been doing solo activities for a long time, so it seems that he is not accustomed to communicating with others.

Well, in the past, there were probably countless idols who lacked such social skills.

Anyway. Who... might you be? Is this the first time you and HiMERU have met?

Aah, thank goodness. After all, "It's forbidden to say 'Nice to meet you' to someone you've already met"!

—Hm, hm. You are Anzu-san?

Ah, HiMERU has heard of you! You're the producer who works for various agencies, aren't you?

HiMERU had hoped to speak with you someday.

...Mm? Why does HiMERU refer to himself as "HiMERU", you ask? Is it strange?

It's not that he has a depersonalization disorder, but for various reasons, HiMERU feels like he is a stranger to himself—

Well, that's an irrelevant matter. And HiMERU doesn't want to delve into it.

—More importantly. Anzu-san, what are you doing in the office this late at night?

Frankly speaking, it's suspicious, and it wouldn't be surprising if someone thought you were a thief!

Fufu. Please don't deny it so desperately, it was only a joke.

It's easy to tell at a glance that you're working overtime because you haven't finished up yet.

HiMERU's specialty is in seeing through disguises. And deduction, too—for various reasons.

However, he's not a policeman or a great detective. Instead, he is an idol. So there's not much point in him having such skills.

—Hm? You're wondering what HiMERU is doing in the office at this hour? You ask if he forgot something?

Fufu. HiMERU thought this may confuse you again, so he didn't want to mention it, but...

The truth is that HiMERU is looking for a ghost that haunts the office night after night.

—Eh? You didn't know?

There are such rumors. Some people say they saw an eerie shadow in the hallway in the middle of the night, or that someone was laughing inside the mirror...

It seems there are heaps of ghost stories similar to those told at schools.

This building has been completely renovated, but the structure itself is quite old...

There are even anecdotes that may or may not be true, such as that it used to be an air-raid shelter during the war, or that it used to be a cemetery.

In such locations, people who feel anxious for some reason may see ghosts.

In psychological terms, it's called a shadow, where stress and fear give an apparition a name and a shape—

However, when there's an unusual amount of rumors being whispered around, they start to appear plausible...

So HiMERU is trying to find out what is really going on here, and he's been doing some intelligence gathering.

—To be precise, he is investigating the reason why such ghost stories have been spreading.

Fufu! See, HiMERU knew you'd react with astonishment! As he predicted!

Well, it can't be helped if you're stunned~ You must think that HiMERU is too old to be doing such tests of courage ♪

Fufu. You're not stunned, you say?

Desperately making excuses such as "it's great to have dreams"... You are a good person, aren't you?

—However, HiMERU is not chasing suspicious rumors just for the sake of curiosity.

"I" don't know how HiMERU would feel, but "I" rather hate the occult.

And yet, there is no smoke without fire...

The reason why people started whispering about ghost stories might be because someone felt a sense of uneasiness about "us" and—

...Oops, HiMERU has talked too much. You're a mysterious person, Anzu-san.

Even though you were right in front of HiMERU, he forgot you were there, and began endlessly rambling on and on, even about things that should be kept a secret.

In that sense, you are like a ghost. Someone who is here, but not quite—

Aah, HiMERU somehow empathizes with you!

"People are attracted to those who resemble themselves" ...HiMERU might fall in love with you ♪

Idol Story 2

Season: Summer

Location: Seisoukan Common Room

~...♪

Mm? O~i, HiMERU-han, what're you doin' this late at night?

—Oh? HiMERU could say the same to you. Are you out on a walk at such a late hour... Oukawa?

Koh koh koh ♪ I'm jus' happy I'm able to walk 'round freely.

Also, when I was at home, I got used to stayin' up late, so sleep don't come to me easy at this time of night.

I keep thinkin' "I oughta go to bed early," but I can't.

That bein' said. If we both don't go to bed soon, it'll have an effect on tomorrow, 'cos human beings can only recover through sleep.

—Is that so. HiMERU's view differs from that, and Shiina, for example, would most certainly hold a different opinion.

Yeah. That moron's a special case in that he can't recover from anythin' that ain't food.

As we spend more and more time together, I've unavoidably learned that he's like a single-celled organism that'll put anythin' and everythin' in his mouth.

He's eatin' all the time. I lose my appetite jus' by lookin' at 'im.

—Fufu. Shiina would surely be unhappy about that, since he seems to love serving food to others.

For real. That guy's like, his whole life's centered 'round his stomach.

Fufu. To HiMERU, that is an enviable thing, to have something so irreplaceable—things like that.

Aah, what does the world look like to such a person?

—Anyhow. To answer your first question, as you can see... HiMERU is writing a reply to a fan letter he received.

Fan retaa? 1

Yes. HiMERU takes pride in his long career history and is a popular idol in his own right.

Letters from fans arrive in boxes, and all of them are piled up here.

Oh? Really, all of these?

Haah... I can't help but sigh. I'm still unfamiliar with idol culture, so this's new to me, y'know?

It's real impressive that they're all handwritten, even in this age of the Internet, ain't it?

Yes. HiMERU is very grateful for it. Even then, these don't include those that were slanderous in nature. The agency censored and removed those.

Huhh~... And despite that, there's still these many left. HiMERU-han must be a real popular guy, huh?

Rather than it being a result of his popularity, HiMERU is one of the rare types of celebrities who reply to letters. His fans are likely to know this, and thus, they send him more and more.

Anyone would be happy to receive a reply to their letter, after all.

...That's right. Communication's only possible when you speak or write and get a proper response.

To communicate with someone can be a pleasure hard to come by. Screamin' into the abyss will only hurt yer throat, and nothin' worthwhile's gonna come of it.

—Oh dear. Oukawa, you look a little hurt. Apologies, did HiMERU unknowingly tread on a sensitive topic for you?

Hm. It ain't good to be too sharp, HiMERU-han.

...No need to worry 'bout it, I'm fine. I was jus' thinkin' 'bout an old pen pal of mine.

Hm. Even though I thought I'd come to terms with it and forgotten 'bout it, the wounds in my heart'll remain unhealed and hurt forever.

Oh, well. Sorry I called out to ya while you were workin', HiMERU-han.

I'm goin' back to my room to rest. When it comes to yer letter writing... there ain't nothin' I can do to help ya, anyway.

Indeed. The senders of the letters, the fans, would most certainly want to receive a reply from their favorite idol written by the person in question.

HiMERU cannot ask someone to write on his behalf, so even if he gets tendonitis, he will have to write them all by himself.

Hmm, "the person in question", huh...?

—What is it?

Nothin'. If you've convinced yerself of that, then I've nothin' to say. ...Jus' be careful not to break yer body or mind by overdoin' it, HiMERU-han.

—Yes. Thank you for your concern, Oukawa.

Goodnight, have a pleasant sleep ♪

Koh koh koh ♪ ...Though that surprise attack was meant to shake ya up, you didn't even tremble a li'l while you were writin'. I gotta praise ya for that.

You've got a calm face and guts, I'm glad I can rely on ya as a unitmate.

Welp. For real now... G'night, HiMERU-han ♪

~...♪

(...Hm. It looks like he's gone. That made me break out in a bit of a cold sweat.)

(It's the same for you. You have a cute face, but I should never be too careless around you... Oukawa Kohaku.)

("Oukawa". I've heard rumors that they have a family history of single-handedly taking care of dirty work...)

(Sure enough, the more someone wants to hide something, the more likely it is for it to reach their ears.)

(But. Even if you heard about the secret that "I'm" carrying, you should've pretended to be unaware of it.)

(And yet, just now, you deliberately gave me a warning, as if to say, "I know"—why?)

(Maybe you were so sleepy that you slipped up, or maybe you didn't mean anything by it...)

(Was it a threat, or a sign of trust as a colleague? I don't know. There's not enough material to speculate, either.)

(Aah, it's really... interesting. Communication, that is.)

(That which is called life! Overflowing with pleasant discoveries and emotions, the most valuable treasure room in this world!)

(—So. "You". You can't keep averting your eyes and pretending you don't see it... Let's get properly involved with the world, "HiMERU".)

Idol Story 3

Season: Summer

Location: ES (Exterior)

~...♪

(—Yup. Successfully made it through the day, HiMERU did a good job once more today ♪)

(Now then. It would be good to go shopping for some daily necessities that haven't been replenished lately, since things were a little hectic for a while.)

(—However, it seems that you can ask the head of the dormitory to get such things for you. In that case, paying out of one's own pocket would be a loss.)

(But putting "me" aside, HiMERU is very particular about what he likes.)

(So that others can bear witness to his "sense of self", should I purchase everything by myself?)

(Though I still have some savings, my current income isn't very high due to a decline in popularity...)

(I also need to pay for HiMERU's medical expenses, so I'd like to avoid any unnecessary spending.)

(Fufu... How distressing, and yet... How pleasant, how pleasant ♪ Keeping the brain cells alive and engaged is the greatest of pleasures!)

HiMERU-shi~ I am so very sorry to stop you! Would you kindly spare me a few minutes of your time?

—Oh. Good evening, Deputy Director.

—Fufu. "HiMERU-shi" somewhat sounds like a greeting from a foreign country.

Pardon? Oh, right! Was that your usual strategy of saying something with a difficult-to-grasp meaning in order to think of a response while the other party's at a loss?

I know the "true nature" of who you are, so such things are unnecessary... HiMERU-shi ♪

—Yes. But in a world where the walls have ears and the doors have eyes, you never know when or where someone might be listening in.

HiMERU has always thought that smartphones are some of the most indispensable tools for spies.

Yes, I think so too! That's why StarPro, or rather, His Eminence Eichi, seems to be controlling the network through the means of "Hold-hands" ♪

—Yes. That man's sharp insight never ceases to amaze. Well, even so, our Deputy Director is no doubt skilled enough to rival him.

Aah, I greatly appreciate your kind words! But there's no need for superfluous social pleasantries. After all, it's just eerie to praise each other among family members~♪2

—Yes. More importantly, what business are you here for? As for HiMERU, he would like to finish his shopping before the store closes, you see?

Yes, yes, I am so terribly sorry about that! I do apologize for being unable to read the air!

...Hmm, well, I stopped you this time to make an important confirmation.

—Important confirmation? Is it okay to talk about it in such an open place?

Rest assured, I will be very careful with the information. I assume HiMERU-shi will do the same~ Since we are both in a difficult situation ♪

—Of course. That's why HiMERU stays under CosPro's care3, because they are "reliable" in that area.

—Anyway. So then, what's the confirmation?

Right. The matter is, HiMERU-shi...

The clever HiMERU-shi may scold me for this, saying something like, "Why didn't you confirm such an important thing earlier?", but...

If things were to go to the extremes, which would you prioritize, "you" or "HiMERU"-shi?

—And by that, you mean?

Let's start with the premise. Right now, you are in an exceedingly unpleasant position.

Well, I am the one who put you in that situation... So you might wonder where I get the nerve to speak of this, but.

Currently, the Crazy:B you belong to is, quite frankly, on the very edge of a cliff.

Personally, and by all means, I want to see you all crawl out of rock bottom, but to be fair, it seems fitting to say that your destiny is like a candle flickering before the wind.

—Yes. Yes, that is unquestionably true.

Of course. And you know, I don't even want to think about that possibility, but...

If by any chance, your Crazy:B fails to show their usefulness, and you all get dismissed...

That is, if the unit is disbanded as agreed, and all the members are fired...

Even in such a worst-case scenario, between "you" and "HiMERU"-shi—it is possible to let one of you keep existing as an idol.

.........

We will reveal the "truth" to the public, and then either "you" or "HiMERU"-shi will be offered up as a human sacrifice to explain the situation away.

Only one of you will be declared an underachiever who failed to become an idol.

We have already prepared a plot that will convince the masses.

It would be a shame to completely abandon HiMERU, an idol with a proven track record, talent, popularity, and competence.

We'd like to keep at least "one of the two".

Of course, we can just choose whichever we prefer to keep, but...

With the cooperation of both of you, it will be easier for us to proceed, and we would also like to know what you'd rather do, just in case.

—Please save "HiMERU".

...Oh my, such an immediate response.

We are not in such a hurry, and it would be fine for you to take it home with you to consider it at your own leisure.

—There is no need for that. In fact, you shouldn't have bothered to even ask.

In this shining and promising idol industry, the one who should remain is, of course... The real one, not the imitation, and the light, not the shadow.

Translation Notes

  1. ↑ Kohaku repeats this in hiragana, indicating his unfamiliarity with the term.
  2. ↑ In other translations, 身内, the word Ibara uses to refer to his and HiMERU's relationship, is translated as "friends" or "idols in the same agency", and while it can also mean that, its primary meaning is relative/family member. Given what gets revealed about them later, I chose to translate it as such, but it's important to note that the ambiguity does exist.
  3. ↑ The expression HiMERU uses, "草鞋を脱ぐ", literally means "to take off one's straw sandals". It means to finish a long journey or to stay at an inn during a journey, which is what HiMERU refers to here.
