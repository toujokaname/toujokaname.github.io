Opening Speech

Everyone, thank you for coming to the party today.

I feel honored that so many people showed up in such a busy end of the year season.

I will make an effort to make this a happy time for all of you who have gathered here.

Well then, please enjoy yourselves...♪

Food Talk

Tatsumi-senpai. Once again, happy birthday...♪

Jun-san, thank you very much. I hope that you enjoy this party.

Yes, thank you so much!

But I mean, are you really havin' a good time? Aren't you always worrying about other people?

Fufu, just the fact that Jun-san came to congratulate me is enough for me...

I know. If you could indulge one selfish request, how about having a cup of tea and talking with me again?

Haha, that's nothin' compared to Ohii-san's selfishness~ If it's okay with you, I'd be glad to ♪

Presents from ES Idols

I never thought I would receive so many gifts... It makes me very happy. Let's open one of them.

Ah, Kazehaya-senpai. That's my present...♪

Aah, so it was from Hajime-san. Thank you very much.

It seems to be very light... Are these tea leaves?

Yes. This is a gift from me inspired by Senpai's image...♪

I couldn't figure out what to pick, so I ended up with something fishy...

That's not true. This is a very special present to me. I will take good care of it...♪

Wishes from ES Idols

Tatsumi-senpai! This is a very nice party. Everybody seems to be having a good time, and the whole place seems filled with happiness.

Yes, it has a pleasant atmosphere. I suppose it's also because of Kazehaya-senpai's personality.

Hiiro-san, Hokuto-san, thank you very much. I am very happy to hear you say that...♪

Ah, happy birthday, Kazehaya-senpai. Let's continue to compete with each other as units of StarPro.

Fufu. Once again, thank you, Hokuto-san. I hope to perform with everyone from Trickstar again ♪

Umu! I'm looking forward to the day when I can sing with Trickstar as well!

Closing Speech

Everyone, did you enjoy today's party?

...Fufu, it feels like I'm asking you to answer, "I enjoyed it." I don't want to force an answer.

At least, I really enjoyed it. I enjoyed the food, the conversation, and the gifts. It made me very happy.

Now, I would like to do my best to return the happiness I have received from you all. I'll be looking forward to it...♪

Party Event

Minitalk 1

Anzu-san. I would like to thank you very much for organizing this party today.

The decorations, the food... I can't thank you enough for having prepared all of this for me.

Anzu-san, please enjoy yourself as well.

Please accept this.

Thank you very much, Anzu-san. Fufu, it's a cute stuffed bear ♪

By the way, I got you a present...

Oh, this is... Is this the stuffed animal that all the birthday people were wearing?

Minitalk 2

Oh, Anzu-san. Can I help you?

Ah, so you came to check on me.

Rest assured, there's nothing to worry about. On the contrary, I am very happy that people from the same office and circles came to congratulate me.

Have you eaten the food?

Yes, I just ate with everyone from ALKALOID.

All the dishes were delicious. Even Aira-san, who has a small appetite, ate a lot today ♪

Have you seen the presents?

No, I was just about to go and see them. Would you like to join me, Anzu-san?

Fufu, I can't wait to see what presents they have for me ♪

Minitalk 3

...Aah, Anzu-san. No, I'm just trying to remember everything about today. It really was a great party.

Not only did I get to celebrate with everyone, but they all seemed to be having a good time.

It was the kind of party that made me feel happy just to be there.

I'm happy you enjoyed it.

Yes, it's a wonderful memory. I'll be able to sleep with a warm feeling tonight.

Next year, too...

Fufu, right now it's too early to talk about next year... I would be happy to celebrate like this again next year ♪

For this purpose, I will continue my efforts as a member of ALKALOID. Anzu-san, thank you for everything you do for me.
